Talks and News

ClimateNLP workshop at ACL 2024

Our paper, "Structuring Sustainability Reports for Environmental Standards with LLMs guided by Ontology", was accepted in the processdings of the first ClimateNLP workshop at ACL. To present our work at the poster session on 16th of August, I flew to Bangkok and attended the entire conference.

Airbus Innvation Day

During the Airbus Innovation Day on March 5th, me and my collegue Rana Abdullah were invited to give a talk about Generative AI and it's application in industry. We also had a tour at the Airbus cybersecurity lab and the Future Industrial Line.

Sustainable AI 2023

Our paper "Reporting and Analysing the Environmental Impact of Language Models on the Example of Commonsense Question Answering with External Knowledge" was accepted at Sustainable AI 2023 in Bonn. On 30th of May I presented the findings of my master's thesis about the environmental impact of LLMs training and fine-tuning, as well as what practices promote transparent and sustainable research.

Computer Science 4 Future

On March 3rd 2023 I was kindly invited by Marin Kohler, the coordinator of Network of Labs, to participate at their LabTalks session. There I presented what potential harmful impact can AI have and what practices promote more mindful and sustainable development.
